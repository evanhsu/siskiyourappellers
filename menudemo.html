Available to Everyone

No eAuth. No Active Directory. No Lotus WebID. No NAP account.
This site is available to every fire crew to help with the administrative record-keeping that they never trained us for. Whether you have an engine, a handcrew, a helicopter, or an IMT - you're invited to sign up for a private record-keeping space that you can selectively share with only the people you want, whether they have a LincPass or not.

Records Management

Consolidate your administrative overhead and enable your entire leadership team to access your record-keeping system in the cloud. You can't trust a spreadsheet once two people each have their own copy, so you're probably keeping a master copy on your own computer, forcing you to do all of the data entry yourself. Stop doing that.
By moving to a cloud-based record-keeping system, you'll be able to deligate administrative tasks more efficiently, provide administrative experience to other members of your crew, and ensure that you're always looking at the most up-to-date info.
