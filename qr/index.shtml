Siskiyou Rappel Crew

The Siskiyou Rappel Crew is highly trained in the tactical and logistical use of aviation assets for fire suppression, and can be rapidly deployed to incidents both Regionally and Nationally.

Our crew consists of 16 firefighters, including our Foreman, Assistant Foreman, two Squad Leaders, two Senior Firefighters, detailers, apprentices and temporary seasonal employees. We are an exclusive use crew designated for Initial Attack, so we maintain our IA readiness at all times. Adaptability is key with missions ranging from wilderness rappel fires to Search & Rescue recon. We also have the ability to assist with extended attack and logistical support missions throughout the lower 48 and Alaska as the need arises.

Our helicopter is carded for rappelling, bucket work, personnel transport, cargo missions, and low-level recon.
